Urban and rural areas face major challenges in managing waste efficiently.  
Traditional systems lack proper monitoring, timely garbage collection, and citizen engagement.  
Due to this:
- Waste piles up, causing health and hygiene issues.  
- Municipal workers have no real-time updates about garbage hotspots.  
- Citizens have no easy way to report unclean areas.  
- Manual record-keeping delays response time.  

There is a need for a **smart, digital waste management solution** that enables **citizens to report, track, and monitor waste issues**, while helping authorities take quick action.
